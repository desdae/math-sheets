Worksheet View Redesign

Three stronger ways to make solving feel clearer.

The current worksheet page works, but the hierarchy is weak. These directions focus on automatic progress saving, a calmer solving surface, easier scanning for wrong versus unfinished answers, and a submit action that feels like a deliberate final step instead of just another header button.

A

Studio + Inspector

Split the screen into a main solving area and a right-side progress inspector. Auto-save status lives in the inspector, and submit moves to a bottom review panel.
